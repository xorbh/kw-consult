# Go-to-Market

## 🛒 Sales & Revenue

- **Lead scoring** – AI models that prioritize which prospects to call first
- **CRM auto-fill** – Automatically log call notes, emails, and next steps
- **Sales forecasting** – Predict pipeline close rates and revenue
- **Proposal generation** – Draft customized quotes/proposals from templates
- **Outbound personalization** – AI-written cold emails tailored to each prospect

## 📣 Marketing

- **Content generation** – Blog posts, social media, ad copy at scale
- **SEO optimization** – AI-driven keyword research and content briefs
- **Email campaign automation** – Segmentation, personalization, A/B testing
- **Customer segmentation** – Cluster customers by behavior for targeted campaigns
- **Chatbots / website assistants** – 24/7 lead capture and qualification

## 🎧 Customer Support

- **AI support chatbot** – Resolve tier-1 tickets automatically (FAQs, order status)
- **Ticket routing & prioritization** – Auto-classify and assign incoming tickets
- **Sentiment analysis** – Flag unhappy customers for proactive outreach
- **Knowledge base generation** – Auto-create help articles from past tickets
- **Voice AI for phone support** – Handle inbound calls without human agents

## 💰 Finance & Accounting

- **Invoice processing** – Extract and classify data from invoices automatically
- **Expense categorization** – Auto-tag transactions from bank feeds
- **Cash flow forecasting** – Predict short-term cash needs
- **Anomaly detection** – Flag unusual transactions or potential fraud
- **Financial report summarization** – Plain-English summaries of P&L, balance sheets

## 🧑‍💼 HR & People Ops

- **Resume screening** – Filter and rank applicants against job criteria
- **Job description writing** – Generate compelling, bias-reduced JDs
- **Onboarding assistant** – Answer new hire questions via chatbot
- **Employee feedback analysis** – Summarize themes from surveys or reviews
- **Scheduling & shift optimization** – AI-driven workforce scheduling

## ⚙️ Operations & Supply Chain

- **Demand forecasting** – Predict inventory needs to reduce over/understocking
- **Vendor management** – Summarize contracts, flag renewal dates, compare bids
- **Process documentation** – Auto-generate SOPs from recorded workflows
- **Predictive maintenance** – For SMBs with equipment, predict failures before they happen
- **Order management automation** – Auto-process, route, and track orders

## 🏗️ Product & Tech (for tech-enabled SMBs)

- **Internal co-pilots** – AI assistants embedded in proprietary tools
- **Bug triage & QA** – AI-assisted test generation and issue classification
- **Feature prioritization** – Analyze customer feedback to surface top requests
- **Documentation generation** – Auto-generate technical docs from code

## 📊 Strategy & Leadership

- **Competitive intelligence** – Monitor competitors, summarize news and reviews
- **Meeting summarization** – Auto-generate action items from Zoom/Teams calls
- **Market research synthesis** – Summarize reports, industry data, trends
- **KPI dashboards** – Natural language querying of business data ("What drove churn last month?")
