# KW Consult

AI-enabled operations consultancy that bridges the gap between AI capability and domain expertise.

We configure, deploy, and monitor AI assistants tailored to specific roles, workflows, and systems within our clients' organisations — empowering subject matter experts rather than replacing them.

## What We Do

- **Discovery & Workflow Mapping** — understand how teams actually operate
- **Agent Design & Configuration** — purpose-built AI assistants for specific roles
- **Integration & Deployment** — wire into existing systems (ERP, CRM, data warehouses, etc.)
- **Monitoring, Tuning & Governance** — ongoing managed service with compliance and audit trails
